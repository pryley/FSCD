Introduction

May 13, 2005

This project and it's design was inspired in part by TheOpenCD.org project, a collection of the best Open Source software. This project attempts to take it one step further to include all of the best, open and closed source, free software: to both inform of, and provide, free alternatives to commonly used commercial software.

We hope you find this resource and information useful, and that it will enable you to become less dependant on commercial software.
