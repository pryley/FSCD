«FotoAlbum 3.4.1

FotoAlbum is a digital photo organizer that allows you to sort organize your photos into albums, sort them, filter them by date, text keywords and more. You can also import pictures directly from your digital camera, a scanner or create a FotoAlbum CD (ISO image or ready-to-burn folder). FotoAlbum displays a handy timeline on top of the interface, which allows you to quickly jump to pictures taken in a certain year. You can add comments and keywords to each image, view them as a slideshow, create a screensaver and more. Additional features include easy emailing and resizing of pictures, support for external editors and more. The software also integrates smoothly with the FotoAlbum online photo storage service, which allows you to create an online web album for family and friends to visit. This feature requires a yearly fee, but is not needed nor required to use the functionality of the album software.

Features:

Import and organize photos and videos

  • Easily import photos into FotoAlbum from a camera, scanner or a photoCD.
  • Automatically generate filenames or use filenames provided by the camera or device.
  • Organize photos and videos into unlimited albums and groups (multi-level grouping)
  • Control the display order of your albums and pictures.

View and update picture properties

  • Assign captions, keywords and dates to your pictures.
  • View and import EXIF information.
  • Use batch mode to update captions, keywords or dates for selected photos.
  • Rotate or flip pictures in single or batch mode.

Browse photos using thumbnails

  • Choose information to display with thumbnails such as caption or date.
  • Customize the appearance of the thumbnails including size and color.
  • Sort thumbnails by date, filename or choose your own custom order.
  • Use filters, custom views and the calendar control to find photos quickly.

View fullsize photos and videos

  • Use the preview window to enlarge the selected thumbnail.
  • View full screen slideshows of your photos and videos.
  • Customize your slideshow with transitions between pictures.
  • Zoom in on picture details in the slideshow viewer.
  • Create a screen saver with your photos.
  • Make your favorite picture your Windows wallpaper.
  • Integrate your favorite tools into the FotoAlbum tools menu.

Share and print photos

  • Create FotoAlbum CD's (CD's with your pictures/videos and FotoAlbum software)
  • Print pictures on your own printer using our convienient page layouts.
  • E-mail photos to friends and family without leaving FotoAlbum.
  • Use Foto-Sync to upload pictures to the web to share with others.

This is the last free version of the software for non-FotoTime subscribers.
