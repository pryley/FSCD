Multimedia > Video Tools > VirtualDub

«VirtualDub 1.5.10

ScreenshotVirtualDub is primarily a linear editing solution; it does not offer general purpose editing capabilities such as splicing or matte effects. It makes an excellent conversion utility though; you can recompress, clip, and filter video to your liking.

ScreenshotVirtualDub has many of the filters you’d expect to find in an image processing program, such as smooth, sharpen, filtered resize, and brightness/contrast adjustment. You can set up multiple filters in a chain, and apply them all in a single pass.

ScreenshotThere are many others filters available that can be downloded for free to extend VirtualDub. The filter being used here is 'Unsharp Mask'.

ScreenshotA lot of filters allow you to preview their effects in realtime. The internal 'Levels' filter applies a levels or levels correction transform to the video.

ScreenshotCurrently installed video codecs are listed in the left pane; select a codec to use or choose "uncompressed RGB" to disable video compression.

ScreenshotOnce an audio codec is chosen, the available compression formats produced by the codec are shown on the right. By default, VirtualDub only displays those formats that can be produced by the codec from the current audio source and filtering settings.
