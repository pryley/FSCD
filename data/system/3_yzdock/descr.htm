System > System Candy > Y'z Dock

«Y'z Dock 0.8.3

Y'z Dock is a program launcher like the dock in MacOSX.
To add a program to the dock, drag and drop a file (.exe, shortcut, etc.) straight from Explorer to the dock area.
To remove a program from the dock, drag and drop the icon out of dock.
Place the PNG images for your icons in the icons folder. Right-click on the icons in the dock, choose 'Property' and choose your icons images there. Right-click anywhere on your dock background and choose 'Setting' to change the settings that apply to the dock itself.

This excellent program is sadly no longer under development.
